YCLUBs 10 Business lines

YCLUB Booking & Sales Marketplace (Q1)

This is the booking and sales marketplace all in crypto.

Utility for YCLUB NFTs

Approximately 30% of the net revenue from the Booking & Sales Marketplace will be distributed to YCLUB NFT olders as rewards. The distribution ratio will be tier based.

When YCLUB NFT holders book a charter, they will receive up to a 3% discount.

Utility for SYC Token

Holders can choose to receive rewards in any of six different crypto currencies: USDT, USDC, ETH, BITCOIN, PAXG and SYC.

When a customer uses SYC for booking, they earn SYC rewards, a type of cash back program

YCLUB Manager™ (Q2)

YCLUB Managers provide brokerage & concierge services to customers booking charters.

The YCLUB Manager program drives value to Epic & Legendary NFTs because to be a YCLUB Manager, one must own one of these NFTs.

Limited to 750 Spots 650 Epics and 100 Legendary).

Approximately 20% of the revenue from the YCLUB Manager program will be allocated to the SYC liquidity pool.

Commissions to YCLUB Managers are 50-70% of YCLUB’s commission and are distributed to Managers in the crypto of their choice.

Worldwide shipping available

YCLUB Store (Q2)

The YCLUB Store is a multi-vendor Web 3 ecommerce marketplace for the yachting industry that will offer erchandise for payment in crypto with a rewards program in the SYC Token.

YCLUB NFT holders receive
additional discounts.

YCLUB NFT holders automatically become YCLUB Affiliates and can earn rewards for bringing new customers to the YCLUB Store.

YCLUB Store vendors must hold and stake 10,000 SYC to sell their goods in the YCLUB Store

Shoppers earn rewards in SYC as they shop.

YCLUB Environmental Initiatives (Q2)

When players in our metaverse win in any of our games,
they can choose to use our PlayToDonate (™) feature and donate all or part of their winnings to
any one of our ocean cleanup partners. When players donate, their funds will be held in YCLUB’s
Environmental Initiatives wallet to be distributed according to the players’ wishes on a
quarterly basis when we will also issue full reports of these distributions.

YCLUBVerse (Q3)

The YCLUBVerse is the first ever
Maritime Metaverse
for Superyachts.

Users need a YCLUB NFT to access the YCLUB Metaverse, (YCLUBVerse)

BAYC and DOODLES can also access our metaverse and both of these communities will get an island in YCLUBVerse

SYC is accepted for use throughout the YCLUBVerse economy.

  • Examples of SYC utilities:
  • Launching & navigating superyachts for SYC rewards
  • Educational games
  • Play and Earn Games
  • Private poker rooms
  • Celebrity Poker Tournaments
  • Sports Betting

YCLUB DEX (Q3)

YCLUB’s DEX is order book based and will include over 25 pairs.

Up to 20% of YCLUB’s net revenue from the DEX will be distributed amongst YCLUB NFT holders.

NFT holders earn SYC rewards for every transaction they make on the YCLUB DEX.

SYC and ETH are the transactional tokens of the YCLUB DEX. The DEX will have over 25 pairs with SYC

Investors will have a chance to earn rewards by participating in the YCLUB liquidity pools.

YCLUB Superyacht Crowdfund (Q3)

YCLUB will offer a unique 3D NFT collection for the YCLUB Superyacht Crowdfund.

Investors buy fractional ownership in the Crowdfund by purchasing an NFT from this unique collection.

Participants in the Crowdfund will earn 60% of the net revenue from bookings of the Crowdfunded Superyacht.

YCLUB Management & Crew Services (Q3)

YCLUB is disrupting the management
and crew services of the
superyacht industry.

Crew members will need to hold a Rare YCLUB NFT to participate in the program.

Additionally, by holding a Rare NFT, crew members will be entitled to participate in the YCLUB rewards distributiton.

Crew may choose to receive payment in any crypto or the SYC Token.

YCLUB crypto/fiat Visa debit card, accepted everywhere, will be offered to crew.

YCLUB NFT Marketplace (Q3)

YCLUB NFTs are purchased and
traded in our YCLUB Marketplace.

The YCLUB NFT Marketplace will feature YCLUB ecosystem NFTs.

There will be no creator’s commission on NFT’s sold in the YCLUB NFT Marketplace and the YCLUB NFT Marketplace commission will be 1%, that is 60% less than the commission fee on OpenSea.

Additionally, Epic & Legendary NFT holders will be able to rent their NFTs for events and other activities that they can not attend.

SYC can be used to purchase NFTs in the YCLUB NFT Marketplace.

holding SYC tokens may give holders certain benefits such as discounts on NFT purchases, or a share of the platform's revenue
