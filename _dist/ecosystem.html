YCLUBs 10 Business lines

YCLUB Booking and Sales Marketplace (Q2)

The Booking and Sales Marketplace is where clientele book charters and buy superyachts.

Utility for YCLUB NFTs

Approximately 30% of the net revenue from the Booking & Sales Marketplace will be distributed to YCLUB NFT holders as rewards. The distribution ratio will be tier based.

When YCLUB NFT holders book a charter, they will receive up to a 5% discount.

Utility for SYC Token

Holders can choose to receive rewards in any of six different cryptocurrencies: USDT, USDC, ETH, BITCOIN, PAXG and SYC.

When a customer uses SYC for booking, they earn SYC rewards

Certified YCLUB Manager (Q2)

Certified YCLUB Managers provide brokerage & concierge services to clientele booking charters or buying superyachts.

The Certified YCLUB Manager program drives value to Super Rare, Epic and Legendary NFTs because to be a Certified YCLUB Manager, one must own one of these NFTs. Limited spots available.

The tier level of the NFT held by Certified YCLUB Managers is one factor in their commission rate.

Approximately 20% of the revenue from the Certified YCLUB Manager program will be allocated to the SYC liquidity pool.

YCLUB Store (Q4)

The YCLUB Store is a multi-vendor Web 3 ecommerce marketplace for the yachting industry that will offer merchandise for payment in crypto with a rewards program in the SYC Token.

Up to 20% of YCLUB's net revenue from the YCLUB Store will be distributed amongst YCLUB NFT holders.

YCLUB NFT holders receive additional discounts.

YCLUB NFT holders automatically become YCLUB Affiliates and earn rewards for bringing new customers to the YCLUB Store.

YCLUB Store vendors must hold and stake 10,000 SYC to sell their goods in the YCLUB Store.

Shoppers earn rewards in SYC as they shop.

YCLUB Environmental Initiatives (Q3)

When players in YCLUB's metaverse win in any of our games, they can choose to use our PlayToDonate feature and donate all or part of their winnings to our Environmental Initiatives. YCLUB will publish reports verifying distribution of these funds for these environmental efforts.

YCLUBVerse (Q4)

The YCLUB Verse is the world's first Superyacht Maritime Metaverse.

Users need a YCLUB NFT to access YCLUB Verse.

BAYC and DOODLES can also access our metaverse and both of these communities will get an island in YCLUB Verse.

SYC is accepted for use throughout the YCLUB Verse economy.

  • Examples of SYC utilities:
  • Launching and navigating superyachts for SYC rewards
  • Educational games
  • Play and Earn Games
  • Private poker rooms
  • Celebrity Poker Tournaments
  • Sports Betting

YCLUB DEX (Q4)

YCLUB’s DEX is order book based and will include over 25 pairs.

Up to 20% of YCLUB’s net revenue from the DEX will be distributed amongst YCLUB NFT holders.

NFT holders earn SYC rewards for every transaction they make on the YCLUB DEX.

SYC and ETH are the transactional tokens of the YCLUB DEX. The DEX will have over 25 pairs with SYC

Investors will have a chance to earn rewards by participating in the YCLUB liquidity pools.

YCLUB Superyacht Crowdfund (Q3)

YCLUB will offer a unique 3D NFT collection for the YCLUB Superyacht Crowdfund.

Investors buy fractional ownership in the Crowdfund by purchasing an NFT from this unique collection.

Participants in the Crowdfund will earn 60% of the net revenue from bookings of the Crowdfunded Superyacht.

YCLUB Management and Crew Services (Q1, 2024)

YCLUB is disrupting the management and crew services of the superyacht industry.

By holding a YCLUB NFT, crew members will be entitled to participate in the YCLUB rewards distribution.

Crew may choose to receive payment in the crypto of their choicelor the SYC Token.

YCLUB crypto/fiat Visa debit card, accepted everywhere, will be offered to crew.
