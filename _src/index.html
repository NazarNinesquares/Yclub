Our mission

Revolutionizing the superyacht industry

Our mission is to build the most robust Web3-based superyacht community in the world, where superyacht customers, owners, industry professionals and enthusiasts can communicate, build relationships and benefit from participating in the superyacht economy together.

YCLUB provides the venues for this community through our Web 3 ecosystem that include

  • a charter sales marketplace
  • a dex
  • in-person events
  • non-expiring revenue-sharing opportunities
  • access to participate in a crowdfunded superyacht project
